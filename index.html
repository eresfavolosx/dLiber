Who are we?
dLiber is a startup company whose main goal is to sell and buy students and professors used or new college supplies at a very cheap price and in an accessible manner. We have a roundtrip during the week visiting several universities and a website to offer our services to our customers no matter where they are.

But above all, to help you generate and save while studying in college.
What do we do?

dLiber focuses on offering university books and other college materials at a fairly low price.

How do we do it?

On-Site
